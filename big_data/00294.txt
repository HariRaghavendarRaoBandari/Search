Coordinates for the Universal Moduli Space of Holomorphic

Vector Bundles

Jørgen Ellegaard Andersen and Niccolo Skovgård Poulsen

March 2, 2016

Abstract

In this paper we provide two ways of constructing complex coordinates on the
moduli space of pairs of a Riemann surface and a stable holomorphic vector bundle
centred around any such pair. We compute the transformation between the coordi-
nates to second order at the center of the coordinates. We conclude that they agree
to second order, but not to third order at the center.

publication.

space in a completely similar way.
