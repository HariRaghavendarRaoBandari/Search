Abstract

We ﬁnd asymptotic equalities for exact upper bounds of approximations by
Fourier sums in uniform metric on classes of 2π–periodic functions, representable in
the form of convolutions of functions ϕ, which belong to unit balls of spaces Lp, with
generalized Poisson kernels. For obtained asymptotic equalities we introduce the es-
timates of remainder, which are expressed in the explicit form via the parameters
of the problem.

Key words: Fourier sums, generalized Poisson integrals, asymptotic equality.
