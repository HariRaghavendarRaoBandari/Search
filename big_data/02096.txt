MEASURE VALUED SOLUTIONS TO THE SPATIALLY

HOMOGENEOUS BOLTZMANN EQUATION

WITHOUT ANGULAR CUTOFF

YOSHINORI MORIMOTO, SHUAIKUN WANG, AND TONG YANG

Abstract. A uniform approach is introduced to study the existence of mea-
sure valued solutions to the homogeneous Boltzmann equation for both hard
potential with ﬁnite energy, and soft potential with ﬁnite or inﬁnite energy, by
using Toscani metric. Under the non-angular cutoﬀ assumption on the cross-
section, the solutions obtained are shown to be in the Schwartz space in the
velocity variable as long as the initial data is not a single Dirac mass without
any extra moment condition for hard potential, and with the boundedness on
moments of any order for soft potential.
