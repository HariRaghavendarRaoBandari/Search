KHALED MEHREZ

Abstract. Our aim is to extend some trigonometric inequalities to Bessel functions. Moreover,
we extend the hyperbolic analogue of these trigonometric inequalities. As an application of these
results we present a generalization of Cusa-type inequality to modiﬁed Bessel function. Our main
motivation to write this paper is a recent publication of Chen and S´andor, which we wish to
complement.

keywords: The Bessel functions, The modiﬁed Bessel functions, Frame’s type inequalities.

MSC (2010): 33C10; 33C15.
