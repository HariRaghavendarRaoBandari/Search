Abstract

In this paper, we studied a Finsler space whose metric is given by an h-

exponential change and obtain the Cartan connection coeﬃcients for the change.

We also ﬁnd the necesssary and suﬃcient condition for an h-exponential change

of Finsler metric to be projective.

Keywords: Finsler space, h-exponential change, projective change.
